Choya Rose

Boutique

Choya Rose is a unique lifestyle and design boutique that offers a curated collection of handerafted furniture, signature home accents and fashion accessories from around Mexico. The diversity of the indigenous cultures, colors and textures of this amazing Country are the primary inspiration our designers use to transform any piece into a one-of-a-kind.

Choya Rose feels proud to collaborate with creative minds and designers from all corners in Mexico embracing their unique free-spirited styles and vision, using natural fibers, materials and textures that are emblematic of each region.

Casa Don Rodrigo is the home of Choya Rose, you can find beautiful jewelry, beach clothing, fun fashion and home accessories, furniture, ceramics, lighting, and unique gifts easy to transport home from Mexico. We have everything you love about Mexico...visit us Monday to Saturday 10 - 9 pm.
